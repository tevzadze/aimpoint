Our Story

The vision of Aimpoint Digital was started when William Miller, Ben Yopp and Ben Strauss were put on an analytics project together while working at a management consulting firm. They all believed that the work they were doing was valuable but were unsure if the client would be able to sustain it once they left. This led them to envision a comprehensive analytics consulting firm that focused on analytics enablement for clients along with analytics execution. They realized that analytics execution is only a short-term fix for companies lacking the analytics infrastructure and strategy. They wanted to help organizations create the right foundation and community to make analytics thrive. 5 years later that vision became a reality when Aimpoint Digital was founded.
